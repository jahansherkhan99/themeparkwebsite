MINI GOLF

Our mini golf courses here at adventure park are fun for all ages! We have four 18 hole fun packed courses that are perfect for your family. Our first course Direction Drive is a quirky address filled course where your whole family can travel the world. Our second course Sports spectacular is perfect for sports loving members of the family. Our third and fourth courses Windmill Way and Paradise Puts are fun scenic routes that everyone in your family will love! Clubs, balls, and scoring cards are all provided and so is the fun!
